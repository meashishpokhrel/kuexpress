SWC:What is it upto?

What Does it do:

The Student Welfare Council(SWC) helps Kathmandu University students pursue higher education by ensuring students the best learning environment around the university.The Student Welfare Council has given aid to hundreds of students years. Many of the recipients are well established and serving in their communities with distinction in various occupations and professions. The Council is extremely proud of years of volunteer service to the graduates Kathmandud University.

Mission:

The missons of SWC:

  • Strengthening the realtion between teacher and students;
  • Helping students to meet their nessecities to establish perfect learning environment around the university;
  • Helping students to raise their voice against the wrong deeds of university student,faculty,staffs or any other problem created within the university premises;
